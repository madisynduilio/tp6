Sweet potato Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup cooked, mashed sweet potato
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ⅓ cup water
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 ⅔ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup chopped pecans

Kitchen Equipment

  • Oven
  • Muffin pan
  • Measuring cups
  • Medium mixing bowl
  • Spoon

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 12-cup muffin pan or line with paper liners.
  3. Combine sugar, sweet potato, olive oil, water, and eggs in a bowl.
  4. Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a second bowl.
  5. Add flour mixture and pecans to the sweet potato mixture.
  6. Fold gently until just moistened, being careful not to overmix.
  7. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes
  9. Remove from oven and cool on wire rack.
